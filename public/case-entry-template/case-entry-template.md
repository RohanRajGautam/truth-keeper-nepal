## Notes for Contributors

1. **Be Factual**: Stick to verifiable facts. Use "alleged" for unproven claims.
2. **Cite Sources**: Every claim should be traceable to a source.
3. **Use Neutral Language**: Avoid inflammatory or biased language.
4. **Respect Privacy**: Don't include personal information beyond what's publicly available.
5. **Update Regularly**: Cases evolve; keep information current.
6. **Nepali Names**: Use correct Nepali spelling and transliteration.
7. **Dates Matter**: Precise dates are crucial for timeline accuracy.
8. **Legal Accuracy**: Be precise about legal status and proceedings.
9. **Ethical Reporting**: Remember this impacts real people and institutions.
